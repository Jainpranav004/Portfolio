# AI Automation Systems
## Overview

A collection of real-world AI automation workflows built using **n8n**, **LLMs**, APIs, and intelligent workflow orchestration to automate repetitive business operations, reporting, communication, and content generation.
These systems focus on reducing manual work, improving operational efficiency, and integrating AI into scalable automation pipelines.

An AI-powered workflow that processes Excel-based financial data, analyzes business performance, generates structured financial insights, and creates downloadable PDF reports automatically.
